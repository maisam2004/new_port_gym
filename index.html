Nothing Hill premier health club. Where time is well spent

a unique package of high-end gym, group exercise classes, swimming, spa, tennis and squash facilities, coupled with a strong social element centred around our Club Lounge. Members can choose to exercise hard or relax and socialise with friends, all in a first class environment.

If you enjoy the motivating and uplifting environment of group fitness classes, portobello has what you are looking for!

Our expert instructors will guide you through workouts designed for fitness progress. Our schedule rotates to include all of your favorites, such as Yoga muscle toning, indoorspin/cycling, Pilates, Cardio Latin, strength training, BODYPUMP, Les Mills, and so much more! We welcome new members and fitness gurus into each of our classes . Find your next fitness class today at Portobello Fitness.
